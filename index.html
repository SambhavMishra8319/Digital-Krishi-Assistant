Empowering Farmers with AI & ML — Get crop suggestions, pest control tips, weather updates & market prices in real time.

Our Digital Krishi Assistant uses the power of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning to bring smart, data-driven solutions directly to the fields. From personalized crop recommendations and early pest detection to accurate weather forecasts and up-to-date market prices, farmers get the right information at the right time. This helps reduce risks, improve yields, and ensure better income — all through an easy-to-use platform designed for every farmer.

About Digital Krishi Assistant

Digital Krishi Assistant is a geospatial application for geo-fencing of agricultural land, geo-tagging of land area and collection of baseline scheme data to ensure the right benefits reach the right beneficiaries. It helps administrators, extension officers and farmers with evidence-based decision making.
